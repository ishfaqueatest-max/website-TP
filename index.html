📖 About This Website

CyberSafe Student Hub is a structured cybersecurity learning platform built specifically for university students. It covers three core threat areas — Malware, Phishing, and Weak Passwords — each presented as a dedicated, self-contained module you can work through at your own pace.

Once you've completed the information sections, a knowledge quiz lets you put what you've learned to the test with scenario-based questions drawn directly from the module content. A built-in progress tracker monitors which sections and the quiz you've finished, giving you a live percentage so you always know exactly how far through the course you are.
